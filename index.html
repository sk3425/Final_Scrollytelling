Flood Protection Funding Cut to Critical Levels

By 2025 Q3, both flood risk funding and the FEMA BRIC program have seen unprecedented cuts, leaving coastal areas vulnerable to climate disasters.

The FEMA Building Resilient Infrastructure and Communities (BRIC) program, which provided crucial pre-disaster mitigation funding, has been completely eliminated in Q2 2025.

Meanwhile, flood risk funding has plummeted to only $200 million - an 86% decrease from its 2022 peak of $1.8 billion.
